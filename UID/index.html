Our Cars



Aston Martin Vulcan

  • Track-only supercar with a naturally aspirated 7.0-liter V12 engine producing over 800 horsepower.
  • Aggressive aerodynamic design and carbon fiber body for enhanced performance.
  • Limited production run of just 24 units, making it highly exclusive.
  • Introduced in 2015 as part of Aston Martin's celebration of motorsport heritage.
  • Includes exclusive track days with professional driver training for owners.

Ferrari F12 Berlinetta

  • Front-engine, rear-wheel-drive grand tourer with a 6.3-liter V12 engine producing 730 horsepower.
  • Accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 3.1 seconds with a top speed of 211 mph.
  • Advanced aerodynamics include active cooling ducts and an integrated rear spoiler.
  • Introduced in 2012 as Ferrari's flagship GT model.
  • Blends performance, luxury, and cutting-edge technology.

Mazda MX-5 Miata MK1

  • Lightweight, rear-wheel-drive roadster introduced in 1989.
  • Iconic pop-up headlights and minimalist design emphasize simplicity and fun.
  • Powers from a 1.6-liter or optional 1.8-liter engine, prioritizing handling over power.
  • Sold over a million units worldwide, becoming the best-selling two-seater sports car.
  • Embodies the "Jinba Ittai" philosophy: horse and rider as one.

Naran Hyper Coupe

  • Limited-production hypercar powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing over 1,000 horsepower.
  • Accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in under 2.3 seconds with advanced all-wheel drive.
  • Sleek, aggressive design inspired by fighter jets for maximum aerodynamic efficiency.
  • Luxurious interior crafted with premium materials for ultimate comfort.
  • Only 49 units will be produced, ensuring exclusivity for collectors.
